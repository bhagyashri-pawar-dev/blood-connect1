About Blood Connect 🩸

Blood Connect is a smart web application created to help people find blood donors quickly during emergencies. It connects donors and receivers in a simple and efficient way.

Users can register, search donors by city and blood group, and contact them instantly. The system also includes a verification process to ensure trust and safety.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to make blood donation easy, fast, and accessible for everyone. We aim to reduce delays during emergencies and help save more lives.

💡 Key Features

⚡ Fast Search

Find donors instantly using filters.

✔ Verified Donors

Trusted donors after verification.

📞 One Click Call

Contact donors directly.

🚨 Emergency Mode

Quick access to urgent donors.
