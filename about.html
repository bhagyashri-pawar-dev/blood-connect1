🩸 About Blood Connect

What is Blood Connect?

Blood Connect is a web-based platform designed to help people quickly find blood donors during emergencies. It connects donors and recipients in a simple and fast way, especially in critical situations.

Why this project?

Every year, thousands of people struggle to find blood in time. This project aims to reduce that gap by creating a system where donors can register and be contacted instantly when needed.

Key Features

  • 🔍 Search donors by blood group and city
  • 🚨 Emergency donor search
  • ✔ Verified donor system
  • 📞 Direct call and WhatsApp contact
  • 🟢 Availability tracking

How it works?

Donors register themselves on the platform with their details. Admin verifies the donors through offline inspection. In case of emergency, users can search and instantly contact available and verified donors.

Future Enhancements

  • 📍 Location-based donor search
  • 🔔 Real-time emergency alerts
  • ☁ Firebase database integration
  • 📱 Mobile responsive design
